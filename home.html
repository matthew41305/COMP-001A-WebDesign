Komodo Dragon Page: Cool Facts

Komodo Dragon's have poisonous Saliva

Komodo dragon's naturally have a poinous saliva that aides in slowing down the prey for the Komodo to catch and kill. Eventually, depending on the weight of the prey, the poison can even pose fatal or completely immobilize the prey. In the past, researchers believed that the reason for the prey collapsing was bacteria from the Komodos bite, however this was incorrect and related to blood loss as well as the effects of venom.

Komodos can run... FAST

Believe it or not, these big reptiles are far from slow, running at around 12 miles per hour, Komodos usually use this speed when catching prey off gaurd, therefore it's usually a short chase as they do not have good longevity when running.
